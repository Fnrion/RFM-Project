## Case Study – Mr. Chen

### Background Introduction

Chinese high-net-worth individuals (HNWIs) are increasingly transferring wealth to Singapore due to capital control tightening, global economic uncertainty, and growing demand for lifestyle and education migration. This case study draws from insights in our market research and highlights how Life Inc can offer personalized planning services to this segment.\
*随着资本管制趋严、全球经济不确定性上升，以及人们对生活方式与教育移民的关注增加，越来越多的中国高净值人士将财富转移到新加坡。本案例基于我们的市场研究，展示了Life Inc如何为该客群提供个性化的财富规划服务。*

Key trends include:

-   **Trust & Asset Protection**: Setting up Singapore-based trusts for intergenerational planning\
    *信托与资产保护：设立新加坡信托，实现代际传承*
-   **Client Service & Immigration**: Using family offices and educational pathways for immigration\
    *客户服务与移民：通过家族办公室和教育路径实现移居*
-   **Cross-Border Tax Planning**: Legally minimizing global tax burdens\
    *跨境税务筹划：合法降低全球税务负担*
-   **Lifestyle & Education**: Ensuring safety, comfort, and top education access for children\
    *生活方式与教育：为家庭带来安全、舒适与优质教育机会*

This project simulates a real-world interaction with a potential client like Mr. Chen, and aims to prepare advisers to engage with Chinese HNW clients in a culturally respectful and financially strategic way.\
*本项目模拟与潜在客户如陈先生的实际交流，旨在帮助顾问以尊重文化和具有财务战略性的方式与中国高净值客户建立联系。*
